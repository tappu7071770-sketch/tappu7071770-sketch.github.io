TURKISH ENERGY

Turkish Energy is a carbonated energy beverage developed for adult consumers who enjoy a refreshing drink experience. This website serves as the official platform for product information, packaging details, and responsible consumption guidance.

500ml Can – Rs. 250
500ml Bottle – Rs. 170

Product Information

Turkish Energy is available in two packaging formats: a 500ml aluminum can and a 500ml plastic bottle.

Both formats contain the same beverage formulation and carbonation level.

The drink is designed to provide a crisp and refreshing taste.

Best served chilled for optimal enjoyment.

Store unopened containers in a cool and dry place away from direct sunlight.

Once opened, consume promptly.

Responsible Consumption Notice

This beverage is intended for adult consumers only.

Not suitable for children.

Pregnant or nursing individuals should avoid consumption.

Do not mix with alcohol.

Excessive intake may cause discomfort.

This product does not replace sleep, meals, or hydration.

Consume in moderation.
